About Halaina

With more than 22 years behind the chair, Halaina has built her salon on personalized service, beautiful color, precision cuts, and long-term relationships with her clients.

Every appointment is tailored to the individual, creating a relaxed experience and beautiful results. Whether you're looking for a fresh haircut, color service, highlights, or a complete transformation, Halaina focuses on helping each client look and feel their best.

Known for her attention to detail and one-on-one service, Halaina continues to serve clients throughout the Myrtle Beach area by appointment only.
